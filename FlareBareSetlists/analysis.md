# Clo's Setlist Pattern Analysis

## Overview
- **18 setlists** analyzed (Oct 2020 - Feb 2026)
- **3-set structure** consistent throughout
- **10-12 songs per set** typical

---

## Set Flow Patterns

### SET 1: "The Jazz Set" (Mellow Opening)
**Purpose**: Ease the audience in with sophisticated jazz standards

**Position 1-2 (Openers)**: Very low intensity, instrumental or classic jazz
- Instrumental opener (various)
- Bye Bye Blackbird
- My Baby Just Cares For Me
- Peel Me A Grape
- Little Boat
- Night and Day

**Position 3-6 (Early-Mid)**: Classic jazz, bossa nova, building warmth
- Turn Me On
- Doralice
- Cheek to Cheek
- Dream A Little Dream
- Love For Sale
- They Can't Take That Away From Me
- Mas Que Nada

**Position 7-10 (Mid-Late)**: Transitioning to soul/R&B
- Afro Blue
- Teach Me Tonight
- One Note Samba
- Who Will Comfort Me

**Position 10-12 (Set 1 Closers)**: Contemporary soul to end set
- Ordinary People
- All That I Can Say
- Stronger Than Me
- Smooth Operator

---

### SET 2: "The Soul Set" (Contemporary R&B/Soul)
**Purpose**: Contemporary favorites, crowd-pleasers, moderate energy

**Typical Songs (flexible order)**:
- All About That Bass (often opens Set 2)
- Feel Like Making Love
- You Know I'm No Good
- Smooth Operator
- I Can't Help It
- Let's Stay Together
- Hypotheticals
- How Deep Is Your Love
- Valerie
- Crazy
- Long Walk / A Long Walk
- What You Don't Do
- Wish I Didn't Miss You
- Not The Only One
- Skyfall
- Say A Little Prayer

**Set 2 Closers**: Building energy for final set
- Feel It Still (PMJ)
- Crazy
- Valerie
- Give Me The Night

---

### SET 3: "The Party Set" (High Energy Finale)
**Purpose**: Get the crowd dancing, end with emotional closer

**Position 1-2 (Party Starters)**:
- Love For Sale (upbeat arrangement)
- You Know I'm No Good
- Mas Que Nada
- Havana
- All About That Bass
- Crazy (Cory Henry arr)

**Position 3-7 (Building Energy)**:
- What You Don't Do
- Wish I Didn't Miss You
- Mr Magic
- Make Me Feel
- Say A Little Prayer
- Frontin'
- Lovefool (Pomplamoose)
- I Will Survive
- Staying Alive

**Position 7-9 (Peak Energy)**:
- Rock With You
- Give Me The Night
- Ain't Nobody
- All Night Long
- Valerie
- Cosmic Girl
- Treasure
- American Boy

**Position 9-10 (Emotional Closer)**:
- At Last
- La Vie En Rose
- Not The Only One

## Song Categories & Inferred Ratings

### VOCAL INTENSITY (1-5 scale)
1 = Easy/Light | 2 = Light | 3 = Moderate | 4 = Strong | 5 = Belt/Powerful

### ENERGY LEVEL (1-5 scale)
1 = Chill | 2 = Mellow | 3 = Moderate | 4 = Upbeat | 5 = High Energy
